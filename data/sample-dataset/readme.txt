This is a sample of 135 images from Cityscapes dataset available at https://www.cityscapes-dataset.com/ and resized by a factor of 4 from 2048x1024 to 512x256 pixels. The sample dataset is for research purposes only in order to reproduce the results of a simple model. It is not a distribution of original dataset and the original data set can't be recovered from this. Please let me know if it breaks the license agreement to remove it immediately.
